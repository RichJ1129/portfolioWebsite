My Background

I am currently taking classes remotely at Oregon State University's Computer Science post-baccalaureate program. My first degree is in Biology from the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth. I have always been a curious person and a person that has been able to take the initiative. In this way I feel like a computer science degree has been right for me. What spurred me to do this program was my old job in assay development in which compiling and analyzing data was a crucial part in making the right decisions. The job taught me how to use excel in a more robust way and how to use minitab in a way that I have never done before. The use of these programs I guess introduced me to learning python so I could analyze data in a better way. I decided I liked to code but was it a career move that I wanted to make. To test the waters I took an online programming class at University of Massachusetts Dartmouth "Computer Program with C", after the end of the class I felt like enrolling in a computer science program was the right choice. This degree will supplement my biology degree by becoming more efficient and allowing me to look at data in different ways. The degree will also open more doors in my career in terms of different software engineering paths.
